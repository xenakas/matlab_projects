 



The folder “Maliars_JBES_21_2003” contains MATLAB software accompanying 
the article "Parameterized Expectations Algorithm and the Moving Bounds"
by Lilia Maliar and Serguei Maliar, (2003), Journal of Business and 
Economic Statistics 21/1, 88-92.

This version: October 30, 2002

The following items are provided: 

1. LICENSE AGREEMENT.

2. The article "Parameterized Expectations Algorithm and the Moving Bounds"
   by Lilia Maliar and Serguei Maliar, (2003), Journal of Business and 
   Economic Statistics 21/1, 88-92 (a working paper version). 

3. MATLAB FILES.

   a. "PEAmbound.m" is the main program solving the one-sector growth models;

   b. "objective.m" is a routine of "PEAmbound.m" computing the objective 
       function. 

For updates and other related software, please, check the authors' web 
pages. For additional information, please, contact the corresponding 
author Serguei Maliar.
